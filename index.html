About Us

A Legacy of Quality Education

Block Takhtgarh, Rupnagar, Punjab, India

Government High School Balewal (GHS Balewal) is a distinguished government-run institution serving the educational needs of students in Block Takhtgarh and surrounding villages of District Rupnagar, Punjab.

Founded with a commitment to providing accessible, high-quality education, GHS Balewal has consistently produced students who excel in academics, sports, and community service. The school operates under the Punjab Government's Department of School Education.

Our Mission

To provide inclusive, quality education that empowers every child with knowledge, values, and life skills for a brighter future.

Our Vision

To be a centre of academic excellence where students from all backgrounds thrive and become responsible, capable citizens of India.
